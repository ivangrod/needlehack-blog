Melissa

The melissa integrationIntegrations connect and integrate Home Assistant with your devices, services, and more. [Learn more] is the main integration to connect to a Melissa Climate A/C control.

There is currently support for the following device types within Home Assistant:

  • Climate

The climate platform will be automatically configured if Melissa integration is configured.
